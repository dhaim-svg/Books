# Cover Brief — *The Skybound Wyrm*

Target: **Kindle eBook cover, 1600 × 2560 px (1.6:1), RGB, JPEG.** Workflow: generate art in
**Nano Banana** (Google Gemini image model) → crop/extend + add title type in **Canva** → export.

Key facts to keep accurate (from `bible/character-profiles.md`):
- **Theron** — 21, a fresh young investigator; middle-class, not a noble; in a traveling coat. Reads as
  earnest and a little stiff. (Carry-through detail: a graduation pin he tugs at — too small for a cover,
  ignore.)
- **The Skybound Wyrm** — a large airship/gondola **drawn by three harnessed drakes** on rigging chains
  (dragon-drawn, not free-flying dragons). Hull "the color of old bone," lacquered, city-block long.
- **The drakes** — big harnessed dragon-beasts: one gray-brown/rust (Mottled), one dark broad-headed
  (Gravel), one smaller pale-gold/cream (Petal). Folded wings, settled in harness — working animals, not
  rampaging monsters. Cozy, majestic, calm.
- **Tone** — cozy, warm, wondrous; gentle mystery. NOT grimdark, NOT action-explosive.

## Nano Banana prompt (paste, then iterate)

> Book cover illustration, vertical 2:3 portrait composition, warm painterly cozy-fantasy style. A grand
> wooden-and-brass airship — a long lacquered gondola the color of old bone — sailing through golden
> late-afternoon clouds above distant mountain peaks. Three large dragon-like drakes in leather harness
> are hitched to the airship by heavy rigging chains, wings folded, pulling it calmly through the sky —
> one gray-brown and rust-colored, one dark and broad-headed, one smaller and pale gold. In the
> foreground, on the open-air promenade deck, a lone young man in a traveling coat stands at the brass
> railing with his back three-quarters to us, gazing out at the drakes. Soft atmospheric haze, rich warm
> light, a sense of wonder and gentle mystery. Leave clear, uncluttered sky in the top third for a title.
> No text, no lettering, no logos, no watermark.

**Iteration tips**
- Generate several; keep the **top third open** for the title.
- Variants worth trying: "tighter on the young man at the rail"; "wider establishing shot of the whole
  airship and drakes"; "dusk / first-bell morning light" for mood.
- Nano Banana may output square — plan to **crop or out-paint to 1.6:1** in Canva.
- Keep the drakes calm and harnessed (cozy), not attacking.

## Canva (title typography)
- Start from a Canva eBook-cover template at **1600 × 2560**.
- Title: **THE SKYBOUND WYRM** (top third). Author: **Theo Weyren** (bottom). Optional small tagline:
  *A Cozy LitRPG Mystery*.
- Legibility test: shrink to ~200 px tall (Amazon thumbnail size) — the title must still read cleanly.
- Export JPEG, sRGB. Save to `publishing/skybound-wyrm-cover-1600x2560.jpg`.
