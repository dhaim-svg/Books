# Chapter–Step Mapping
*Skybound Wyrm · ~40 chapters · ~100k words · ~2,500 words/chapter*

Steps 6 and 10 each get 4 chapters (structural hinges with required breathing room).
Step 12 gets 5 chapters (Quest Resolution + System Reveal + Denouement + Geographic Click + Book-2 Thread).

- Never two plants in the same scene (different beats in the same chapter = fine)
- Niece-softness saved for last of Act 1 (Ch 11) — closest to the reveal
- Kindnesses spread across Steps 1, 3, 4 for even texture
