# Thank You for Reading {.unnumbered}

Thank you for flying the Wyrm route with Theron.

If you enjoyed *The Skybound Wyrm*, the single most helpful thing you can do is
leave a short review on Amazon. Reviews — even one line — are what help other
cozy-mystery and LitRPG readers find the book, and they genuinely make the
difference for a new author. It takes a minute, and it means a great deal.

# About the Author {.unnumbered}

Theo Weyren writes cozy mysteries with a System running quietly in the background —
stories where the puzzle matters, the violence stays offstage, and the people are
worth the trip. *The Skybound Wyrm* is the first.
