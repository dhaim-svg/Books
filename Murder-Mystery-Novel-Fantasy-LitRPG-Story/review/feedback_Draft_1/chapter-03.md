The "he couldn't tell whether the System was being dry on purpose" beat at the chapter's close confirms he has a sense of humor about his own condition. 100,000 words with him: yes.

### 4. Tone calibration

Solidly cozy throughout. The Wisp scene is the warmest passage in the chapter — the three-drake character sketches (Mottled performing when unwatched, Gravel performing when watched, Petal just working) carry the book's texture without feeling like a worldbuilding lecture. The manifest scene runs procedural in its middle section, which reads flat rather than dark, but it earns its place because the restraint-as-labor theme is the chapter's emotional content. The closing is exactly the right register: concrete, quiet, no thematic gloss. No moments felt too dark or too jokey.
