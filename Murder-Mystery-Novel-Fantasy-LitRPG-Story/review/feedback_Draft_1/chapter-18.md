The only moment that approached something heavier — Sable's final line about Joren's family — lands with exactly the right weight: one quiet sentence, absorbed, not dwelt on. The closing image (the harbor water, the unlit lantern, patient) is the right shape. Register is consistent.

**Keep:** "He could not argue with this, which was probably why she said it that way." Precise, dry, warm. Shows both characters in one sentence without editorializing. Earns its place.
