### 5. Line-level

**Cut:** "which was the gap between his method and hers that had not closed over the course of nine chapters and was probably not going to close today." This line is the author reaching through the page to remind the reader of the arc. "Nine chapters" is a production artifact, not story-world knowledge. The observation about their respective strengths is earned by the scene itself; the meta-count undercuts it.

**Keep:** "He said it like — like it was a standard precaution. Like someone had already asked about him before, and it had not been academic." The doubled "like" earns its hesitation, and "had not been academic" does the work of an entire sentence in four words. Keep exactly as written.
